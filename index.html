About Me

Download My CV

Hi! I'm Parthasaarathy Sudarsanam, a passionate researcher currently pursuing my doctoral degree at the Audio Research Group, Tampere University, Finland. My work focuses on multimodal machine perception, where I aim to develop machine learning models that can interpret both audio and visual information in a way that mirrors human perception. Additionally, my research delves into sound event detection and localization, and I have been actively involved in organizing the annual DCASE challenge in this field.

Prior to this, I worked as a Senior software engineer in deep learning at Sony India Software Center in Bangalore, India. My research in auditory intelligence included projects such as speech source separation, voice conversion, and music source separation in collaboration with the Audio Technology Department of Sony Corporation, Tokyo.

Outside of work, I enjoy playing chess and badminton, cooking Indian food, and exploring the beautiful Finnish nature.
